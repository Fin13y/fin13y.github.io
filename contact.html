Driving Directions

From Downtown: Take Main Street north for 2 miles. Turn right on Garden Lane. We're the third building on the left with the large greenhouse out back.

From Highway 50: Take Exit 23 (Oak Street). Head east for 1.5 miles, then turn left on Garden Lane. We're about half a mile up on the right.

Parking: Free parking available in our lot. Accessible parking spaces are located near the entrance.

Accessibility: Our greenhouse is wheelchair accessible with wide aisles and accessible restrooms. Please let us know if you need assistance, and we're happy to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to call ahead before visiting?

Not necessary! We welcome walk-ins during our regular hours. However, if you're looking for a specific variety or large quantities, calling ahead helps us ensure we have what you need in stock.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and debit cards. We also offer discounts for bulk purchases and special pricing for community gardens and schools.

Can I bring my kids?

Absolutely! We love having young gardeners visit. Many children are fascinated by the greenhouse and learning where plants come from. We just ask that parents supervise their children around the plants.

Do you offer any classes or workshops?

We occasionally host free workshops on topics like transplanting, organic pest control, and season extension. Check our blog for announcements, or ask when you visit. We're also always happy to give informal advice!

What if my seedlings don't survive?

We stand behind our plants. If seedlings fail within the first week due to plant health issues (not environmental factors), bring your receipt and we'll replace them. We want you to succeed and will work with you to figure out what went wrong.

We'd Love to See You!

Whether you're starting your first garden or you're a seasoned grower, we're here to help. Stop by the greenhouse, and let's talk plants!
